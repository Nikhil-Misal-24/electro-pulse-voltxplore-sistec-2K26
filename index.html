GAME STRUCTURE

Round 1: Visual Instinct (Identify & Ignite)

This high-speed preliminary round tests your ability to recognize the building blocks of electronics. Participants must identify components from macro-photography, distinct hardware sounds, or short technical clips.

  • Responses must be submitted within the allotted 20-second window per slide.
  • Points are awarded based on both accuracy and response time.
  • The top-scoring teams will qualify for the subsequent round.
  • The use of external aids or internet-enabled devices is strictly prohibited.

Round 2: The Tech-Trail (Technical Hunt)

A department-wide scavenger hunt that merges logic with navigation. Teams must decode technical riddles to locate specific laboratory equipment or hidden "flags" within the ECE wing.

  • Each team starts with a primary clue; subsequent clues are earned only after solving a technical puzzle at each station.
  • Teams must collect the maximum number of flags within the 45-minute time limit.
  • Interference with other teams or department property will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Only the top 5 teams with the highest flag count will proceed to the Grand Finale.

Round 3: The Debugging Challenge (Circuit Conquest)

The ultimate test of practical troubleshooting. Teams are presented with a malfunctioning circuit containing intentional errors—such as incorrect polarities, dry solders, or misplaced connections—and must restore full functionality.

  • All necessary diagnostic tools (Multimeters, breadboards, and basic components) will be provided.
  • No external schematics or mobile data usage is allowed during the debugging process.
  • Winning Criteria: The first team to achieve the verified output will be declared the champion.
  • In the event of a tie, the team that used the fewest replacement components will win.
